Thank you for visiting aCATServer Portal. By accessing and using this website, you are deemed to have accepted to be legally bound by these Terms of Use. If you do not agree to these Terms of Use, please discontinue using this website immediately.

Personal Data We Collect

aCATServer Portal collects data from you, through your interactions with our services. You provide this data directly. The data we collect depends on the context of your interactions with aCATServer Portal and the choices you make, including the services you use.

You have choices when it comes to the data you share. When we ask you to provide personal data, you have all the rights to decline. Most of our services require some personal data to provide you with a resolution. If you choose not to provide data required to provide you with a resolution, you cannot use those services. Likewise, when we need to collect personal data by law to enter or carry out a contract with you, and you do not provide the data, we will not be able to enter the contract; or if this relates to an existing service you are using, we will have to suspend or cancel it. We will notify you if this is the case at the time. Where providing the data is optional, and you choose not to share personal data, features such as personalisation in responses that use such data will not work for you.

How We Use Personal Data

aCATServer Portal uses the data we collect to provide you with rich, interactive experiences. Particularly, to:

  • Provide our services, which includes updating tickets and providing support. It also includes the sharing of data, when it is required to provide the services or carry out the transactions you requested.
  • Improve and develop our services.
  • Personalise our services and make recommendations.

We also use the data to carry out activities, which includes analysing our performance, meeting our legal obligations, developing our team and doing research.

In carrying out these purposes, we combine data we collect from different contexts (for example, from your use of the aCATServer Network Ticket System) to give you a more enjoyable server experience, to make informed activity decisions and for other legitimate purposes.

Our processing of personal data for these purposes includes both automated and manual (human) methods of processing. Our automated methods often are related to and supported by our manual methods. For example, our automated methods include the automatic consolidation of all responses submitted from the use of the aCATServer Network Ticket System.

Reasons We Share Personal Data

We share your personal data with your consent to complete any transactions or provide any services you have requested or authorised. We also share data when required by law or to respond to legal process; to protect our server members; to protect lives; to maintain the security of our services and protect the rights and property of aCATServer Portal and its users.

How to Access and Control Your Personal Data

You can also make choices about the collection and use of your data by aCATServer Portal. You can control your personal data that aCATServer Portal has obtained, and exercise your data protection rights, by creating a case using the aCATServer Network Ticket System. In some cases, your ability to access or control your personal data will be limited, as required or permitted by applicable law. How you can access or control your personal data will also depend on which services you use. For example, you can:

  • Request for aCATServer Portal to clear some of your data through the aCATServer Network Ticket System.
