aCATServer Network is intended to be a place that is both fun and friendly for everyone to play on. Hence, players are expected to adhere to the guidelines listed on this page. By playing on the aCATServer Network, you agree to be bound by these guidelines.

Thank you for playing your part to keep the server healthy and promote positivity among the community.


Last amended: 9 December 2020 (21:43)
S/N Rules Description Sanctions for Misconduct
1 No cheating Cheating is defined as the use of illegal clients, mods or resource packs that gives a player an unfair advantage over other players.

1st Offence Stern warning

2nd Offence 14 days ban

Subsequent Offence Permanent ban
2 No sanctions evasion Sanctions evasion, or punishment evasion, is defined as an attempt by an individual or group to avoid sanctions by using all sorts of methods possible.

1st Offence Stern warning in addition of sanctions imposed

2nd Offence 7 days ban

Subsequent Offence 14 days ban per count of misconduct
3 No submitting fake report Submitting a fake report is defined as an act of submitting falsified or forged information via the e-form.

1st Offence Stern warning

2nd Offence 7 days removal of reporting privilege

Subsequent Offence Permanent removal of reporting privilege
4 No scamming Scamming is defined as an attempt to trick another player into giving out their personal information or inventory items without them knowing their motive.

1st Offence Stern warning

2nd Offence 14 days ban

Subsequent Offence Permanent ban
5 No doxxing Doxxing is defined as publishing someoneâ€™s personal information with the intention to harass. Depending on the severity, a police report may be lodged against the offender(s). If a report is lodged, you may be charged by the law of Singapore under the Protection from Harassment Act (Chapter 256A).

Minor Offence 14 days ban

Serious Offence Permanent ban and police report lodged against the offender(s)
6 No griefing Griefing is defined as using aspects of the game in unintended ways with the intention to irritate or harass other players.

1st Offence Stern warning

2nd Offence 14 days ban

Subsequent Offence Permanent ban
7 Respect all players Every member playing on aCATServer should respect each other no matter what at all times.

1st Offence Warning

2nd Offence 7 days ban

Subsequent Offence 7 days ban per count of misconduct

aCATServer Network reserves the right to amend the guidelines listed on this page at any time without prior notice.
