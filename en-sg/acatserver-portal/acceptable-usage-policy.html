aCATServer Network Acceptable Usage Policy

Last Updated: 9 June 2021

By accessing and playing on the aCATServer Network, you are deemed to have accepted to be legally bound by this Acceptable Usage Policy. If you do not agree to this Acceptable Usage Policy, please do not access, join or play on the aCATServer Network.

  1. Communications via the aCATServer Network shall not contain materials that are fraudulent, distasteful, designed to harass, embarrassing, sexually explicit, obscene, intimidating, defamatory, or which incites religious or racial intolerance or are otherwise deemed inappropriate by aCATdemy. Any material that violates Singaporeâ€™s laws and regulations shall not be transmitted to the Internet. This includes, but not limited to, copyrighted materials, threatening or obscene materials, or materials protected by trade secrets.
  2. Members of the aCATServer Network must not engage in malicious electronic activities, including, but not limited to the following:
    • unauthorised access to computing resources;
    • masquerade as another account-holder;
    • circumvent security systems;
    • exploit or probe for security holes in the networks;
    • attack or degrade the system and network performance, cause damage to aCATServer Network;
    • install programs such as Trojans or rootkits into any IT resources;
    • intentionally introduce computer viruses, worms, trojan horses or any malware into any computer or networks;
    • read, forge, alter or delete any messages or network communication without authorisation;
    • use any software and hardware, such as traffic generators, network sniffing tools, remote administration tools, diagnostic and vulnerability scanning tools, which can compromise the security or performance of the systems as well as the networks.
    • allow personal computing devices to route or bridge traffic from other external networks to the traffic of aCATServer Network;
    • share account and password to other parties to gain access to aCATServer Network; and
    • break any guidelines set by aCATServer Network.
  3. Members of the aCATServer Network shall take adequate measures to ensure that their computing devices that are connected to aCATServer Network systems and networks are adequately protected to prevent unauthorised users from using their devices to access aCATServer Network systems and networks illegally. These measures include, but not limited to the following:
    • using strong passwords/passcodes and changing them regularly;
    • installing security programs such as antivirus and firewall;
    • patching device operating systems and application software regularly;
    • rebooting personal computing devices regularly so that upgrades and patches can take effect;
    • setting on auto-lock timeout;
    • reporting security incidents to aCATdemy or the staff members of aCATServer Network.
  4. This Acceptable Usage Policy provides the main guidelines under which IT resources are to be used when members play on the aCATServer Network. Though they are exhaustive, these are all meant to ensure that the computing environment remains safe for all members and not abused by any person. Any ill-intent activity on the systems and networks of aCATServer Network would be subject to disciplinary action and involvement with the Singapore Courts. Such access to the systems and networks of aCATServer Network would be immediately suspended, pending further investigations by aCATServer Network if members or their computing/mobile devices are suspected to have violated this Acceptable Usage Policy.
