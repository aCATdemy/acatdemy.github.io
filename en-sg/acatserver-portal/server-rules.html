SERVER RULES

aCATServer is intended to be a fun and friendly environment for everyone to play on. Hence, I have created a list of rules which I expect all players to uphold at all times. By playing on aCATServer, you agree to be bound by the rules listed on this page.

I foreseen aCATServer being a positive and respectful community where negativities cease to exist. By following these simple rules, we can all do our part to make aCATServer a place for everyone to have fun and enjoy themselves.

Rules Description Sanctions for Misconduct
No cheating Cheating is defined as the use of illegal clients, mods or resource packs that gives a player an unfair advantage over other players.
  • 1st Offence Stern warning
  • 2nd Offence Two weeks ban
  • Subsequent Offence Permanent ban
No sanctions evasion Sanctions evasion, or punishment evasion, is defined as an attempt by an individual or group to avoid sanctions by using all sorts of methods possible.
  • 1st Offence Stern warning in addition of sanctions imposed
  • 2nd Offence One week ban
  • Subsequent Offence Two weeks ban per count of evasion
No submitting fake report Submitting a fake report is defined as an act of submitting falsified or forged information via the e-form.
  • 1st Offence Stern warning
  • 2nd Offence Revoke of reporting privilege for one week
  • Subsequent Offence Revoke of reporting privilege permanently
No scamming Scamming is defined as an attempt to trick another player into giving out their personal information or inventory items without them knowing their motive.
  • 1st Offence Stern warning
  • 2nd Offence Two weeks ban
  • Subsequent Offence Permanent ban
No doxxing Doxxing is defined as publishing someone’s personal information with the intention to harass. Depending on the severity, a police report may be lodged against the offender(s). If a report is lodged, you may be charged by the law of Singapore under the Protection from Harassment Act (Chapter 256A).
  • Minor Offence Two weeks ban
  • Serious Offence Offenders handed over to law
No griefing Griefing is defined as using aspects of the game in unintended ways with the intention to irritate or harass other players.
  • 1st Offence Stern warning
  • 2nd Offence Two weeks ban
  • Subsequent Offence Permanent ban
Respect all players Every member playing on aCATServer should respect each other no matter what at all times.
  • 1st Offence Warning
  • 2nd Offence One day ban
  • Subsequent Offence One week ban per count of violation

aCATServer reserves the right to amend the rules and sanctions at any time. Under special circumstances, sanctions may be given by the moderation team at their own discretion.
