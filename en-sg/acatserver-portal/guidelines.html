Server Guidelines

aCATServer Network aims to be a fun, welcoming and friendly environment for members to play and bond. As such, I have painstakingly crafted a list of guidelines that I expect all members to follow with no exceptions.

One of my goals is for aCATServer Network to be a positive and respectful space where cheating, inappropriate content, bad player behaviour and other negative actions are kept to a minimum. By following these simple guidelines, we can all help make aCATServer Network a great place to have fun with one another.

By choosing to play on the aCATServer Network, you are deemed to have agreed to follow all the guidelines listed below.

S/N Guidelines Definition Sanctions for Misconduct
1 No cheating Cheating is defined as the use of illegal clients, mods or resource packs that gives a player an unfair advantage over other players.
  • 1st Offence Stern Warning
  • 2nd Offence [Seven days] Account/IP address suspension
  • Subsequent Offence [Permanent] Account/IP address suspension
2 No sanctions evasion Sanctions evasion, or punishment evasion, is defined as an attempt by an individual or group to avoid being sanctioned using all sorts of methods possible, including but not limited to bribery and avoiding account/IP suspension by leaving the server.
  • 1st Offence [One day] Account/IP address suspension + imposed sanctions
  • 2nd Offence [Three days] Account/IP address suspension + imposed sanctions
  • Subsequent Offence [Five days] Account/IP address suspension + imposed sanctions
3 No submitting fake report Submitting a fake report is defined as an act of submitting falsified or forged information via the e-form.
  • 1st Offence Stern warning
  • 2nd Offence [Seven days] Revoke of reporting privilege
  • Subsequent Offence [Permanent] Revoke of reporting privilege
4 No scamming Scamming is defined as an attempt to trick another player into giving out their personal information or inventory items without them knowing their motive.
  • 1st Offence Stern warning
  • 2nd Offence [Seven days] Account/IP address suspension
  • Subsequent Offence [Permanent] Account/IP address suspension
5 No doxxing Doxxing is defined as publishing someoneâ€™s personal information with the intention to harass. Depending on the severity, a police report may be lodged against the offender(s). If a report is lodged, you may be charged by the law of Singapore under the Protection from Harassment Act (Chapter 256A) .
  • Minor Offence [14 days] Account/IP address suspension
  • Major Offence [Permanent] Account/IP address suspension + lodging of police report against the offender(s)
6 No griefing Griefing is defined as using aspects of the game in unintended ways with the intention to irritate or harass other players.
  • 1st Offence Stern warning
  • 2nd Offence [Seven days] Account/IP address suspension
  • Subsequent Offence [Permanent] Account/IP address suspension
7 Respect all players All member playing on the aCATServer Network should respect each other at all times.
  • 1st Offence Stern Warning
  • 2nd Offence [One day] Account/IP address suspension
  • Subsequent Offence [Three days] Account/IP address suspension
aCATServer Network reserves the right to amend the Server Guidelines at any time without prior notice.
